Our Story

It all started on a rainy evening in a small coffee shop in Dublin. Sarah, engrossed in her book, barely noticed David until he accidentally spilled his latte on her. What began as an awkward apology quickly turned into a charming conversation.

From that day on, shared laughter, late-night talks, and countless adventures brought them closer. They explored the Cliffs of Moher, danced to traditional Irish music, and discovered a mutual love for baking.

David proposed on a beautiful sunset cruise on the River Liffey, a moment they will cherish forever. Now, they are excited to embark on their greatest adventure yet: marriage, surrounded by their loved ones.
